The Queency Academy

The queency is an institute for computer training.The institute is owned and managed by a woman of high level of dedication ,commitment and inspiration a woman by name Mrs Esther Francis the CEO of the academy .The institute is located at Akwa Ibom and in Oron Local Government Area .Its residence is in 308 oron road ,and also a branch at marina for easy acess to those staying around that area . The existence of the institutes has given chance to young school leavers to acquire knowledge about computer ,most especially this new era where 90% of our daily activities are done electronically. The foundress being a lover of education brought the establishment of the queency academy to help develop young people on the use of computers and so on.The academy has produced thousands of computer literates all over the state which has made our generation progress well in this electronic age.Young school leavers are highly recommended to make good use of the opportunity granted unto them for the proper development of the country
